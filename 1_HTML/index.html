Frontend Forge

A social platform for web developers and designers

Frontend Forge is a social platform for web developers and designers. Create a profile, save and share projects, get feedback with likes and comments, participate in forums – and showcase your portfolio publicly for employers.

Roadmap

Frontend Forge Plan

Core Platform

Profiles, projects, likes, comments and forums.

Community and Moderation

Better profiles, groups and community tools.

Intelligent Matching

Suggestions for projects, people and content (AI).

Collaboration at Scale

Team features, workflows and portfolio for employers.

Laying the Foundation

MVP in development

The focus of the first version is clear: profiles and portfolios, projects with sharing, likes, and comments, as well as a simple forum for discussions. This creates a solid foundation – professional, yet designed for creative people who want to shape a digital future together.

This is not just a platform – it’s a foundation for the creative coders of the future. A base where innovation meets collaboration, where individual visions become collective power.
